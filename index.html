What is Maritan Doge?

Maritan Doge is the meta-universe of the Mars modal cryptocurrency space. It is inspired by Musk's Mars mania: the future economic model of Mars will be based on cryptocurrency. In tribute to Musk and DOGE, Martian doge was born, aiming to be the necessary economic application tool for future human landing on Mars.
Martian doge ecological model is divided into three main parts: Martian doge NFT, Martian doge mining ecology and Martian doge wallet, and will develop NFT GAMEFI in the future

  • ●  Application development: the applications we develop will be used in the economy and society of the Mars world.
  • ●  Martian Foundation: The $MAD Martian Foundation will be built to help humanity breakthrough technology, and will also provide support services for future projects.
  • ●  Ecology: Martian doge ecological model is mainly divided into three main parts Martian doge NFT, Martian doge pledge mining and Martian doge wallet, GAMEFI future release.

Martian doge wallet

Martian doge wallet, the purpose of Martian doge wallet is to integrate more people into our ecosystem, that is to say, Martian doge wallet is an aggregator, the future ecology of Martian doge will continue to expand and will be reflected in Martian doge wallet, holding Martian doge will be able to register, login and use, which is the third step of our current This is the third step of our ecological structure

Advantages of Martian doge wallet

  • Allow users to place bets, trade or participate in games integrated into the wallet using tokens stored directly on the wallet.
  • Uses a private key stored on the user's device, we cannot control the private key for any user.
  • Easy-to-use, user-friendly interface for trading on PCs and cell phones.
  • Support for multiple tokens with different blockchains.
  • Users are in full control of their cryptocurrency and can trade anytime, anywhere
